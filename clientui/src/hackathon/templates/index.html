About HomeIE

HomeIE seeks to help people in Ireland transition from the insecurity of the private rental market into secure long-term housing. Given your personal circumstances, it helps you to understand some options, to progress to a longer-term housing solution, and spells out some steps you may need to take to do so.

HomeIE recognises that secure housing could be:

  • Purpose-built or publicly owned social housing, under a local authority rental scheme (i.e. not a private landlord)
  • A property purchased by you (could be part funded through grant schemes, or co-owned via a Shared Ownership scheme)

FAQ

Imagine trying to buy a house. You’re bombarded with information—mortgage rates, property prices, legal jargon—all spread across different platforms. It’s not just confusing; it’s downright frustrating. Many are left feeling powerless, uncertain, and overwhelmed.

Enter Homie, our smart AI assistant. Homie isn’t just another chatbot; it’s a comprehensive, intuitive platform that consolidates all the crucial information you need to secure a home. Whether it’s finding the right mortgage, understanding the fine print, or discovering financial assistance programs, Homie’s got your back. Powered by the latest AI technologies like ChatGPT-4 and Gemini, Homie provides personalized advice, tailored to your financial situation, preferences, and needs.

Our datasets are carefully curated, pulling from reliable sources to ensure Homie’s advice is accurate and trustworthy. We’ve considered everything, from current market trends to localized housing data.
In line with the theme “AI Here for Good,” we’ve embedded ethical considerations into every layer of HomeIE. Our AI doesn’t just analyze data; it respects your privacy, ensuring that no personal information is retained. We’ve implemented fairness checks to prevent bias, making sure that Homie serves everyone equally, offering advice that’s as diverse as our user base.
