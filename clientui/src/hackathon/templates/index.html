About HomeIE

HomeIE seeks to help people in Ireland transition from the insecurity of the private rental market into secure long-term housing. Given your personal circumstances, it helps you to understand some options, to progress to a longer-term housing solution, and spells out some steps you may need to take to do so.

HomeIE recognises that secure housing could be:

  • Purpose-built or publicly owned social housing, under a local authority rental scheme (i.e. not a private landlord)
  • A property purchased by you (could be part funded through grant schemes, or co-owned via a Shared Ownership scheme)

Disclaimer

HomeIE aims to assist you by using AI to automatically gather information which appears to be relevant to your circumstances. Given that AI is an emerging technology, any information shown cannot be guaranteed to be correct, and the responsibility remains with you, the user, to verify that information with the source provider.

Having said that, this is a learning model so if you spor an error please let us know on (contact-us details) and we will make every effort to correct it!

Data Protection

Any data you enter (income, outgoings, work situation, etc) is retained by HomeIE for the duration of the session only. However, this data is submitted to a commercial LLM platform and we actually haven't a clue whether they are going to grab your data and punish you with it forever. Sorry about that.
